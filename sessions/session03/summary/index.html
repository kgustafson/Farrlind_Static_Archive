Canon Events

  • The party engages in combat with a fey witch who summons two goblins. [Fey Woods] (combat)
  • Roon and Corvinas quickly take down one of the goblins. [Fey Woods] (combat)
  • The witch counters with magic missiles, brings Faban to an inch of death, and then turns invisible. [Fey Woods] (combat)
  • Gildas casts grease where the witch was last seen, Makani kills the remaining goblin with spiritual weapon, and Corvinas lays hands on Faban to bring him back to health. [Fey Woods] (combat)
  • The witch reappears near Gildas, strikes Roon to death's door, and is killed when Corvinas cleaves her head from her body; her remains turn to dust. [Fey Woods] (combat)
  • The witch leaves behind a satchel that appears empty but radiates illusion and aberration magic; after the party pokes it with a stick and Faban throws in a copper piece, the bag releases souls and an ancient necromantic tome that Gildas examines. [Fey Woods] (discovery)
