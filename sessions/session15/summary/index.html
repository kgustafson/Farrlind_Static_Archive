Session 15: Coastal Aftermath

Canon Summary

  • Physical date: 2025-12-21
  • In-game date: 1832 AS Namal 11
  • Primary location: Paramon
  • Review status: applied, applied 2026-05-05

Canon Events

  • The party returns to Paramon beneath heavy mist after the shoreline battle, finding the coast partly evacuated and bodies being gathered. [Paramon] (travel)
  • Corvinas examines the remnants of the water elemental and concludes that it was likely a guardian bound to place, with something else riding or using it. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • Faban performs The Defense of the Watery Dunes for the shaken people of Paramon, framing the recent battle as resistance rather than terror, and earns thirteen gold in tips. [Paramon] (social)
  • Brigit finds drink and disappears briefly into a local watering hole. [Paramon] (social)
  • Gildas notices the sigil of a fallen chaos elemental, while Corvinas observes two figures speaking before one disappears. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • Faban notices the disappearance as a distortion, uses dancing lights and Tasha's cruel laughter to reveal a black-furred tabaxi, and forces him to speak. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • The tabaxi speaks of ancient dragonlands, Tiamat, and demons preparing for resurrection; he implies the Cataclysm is a requirement rather than merely a consequence. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • Faban lets the tabaxi go after questioning him and tells the others a simpler story that the tabaxi escaped. [Paramon] (social)
  • The party returns to the Well and finds raw arcane force thrumming through the chamber, unshaped and dangerous. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • The party realizes that the Well was most likely unleashed by the Wand of Wells and that the Well is no longer sentient. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • Corvinas recognizes himself as the conflict within the Well, power surges through him, and a psychic backlash strikes the party before Corvinas remains standing. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • Mikani and Roon visit the Temple of Namaloa, where Iron Paw reacts angrily to mention of the Celestial Isles, refuses questions about the Well, expels them, and is revealed as wearing borrowed colors rather than being truly Namaloan. [Paramon] (discovery)
  • Gildas speaks with a sapphire-eyed stranger in a tavern and mentions the detail only once afterward. [Paramon] (social)
  • Faban performs that night, and the innkeeper grants free room and board. [Paramon] (social)
