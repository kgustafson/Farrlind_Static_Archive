Canon Events

  • During the journey north, Corvinas studies a book about the archaeological dig site and learns it played a pivotal role in the ancient transition from the Era of Dragons to the Era of Gods. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (discovery)
  • The caravan travels north for about a week without encounters. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (travel)
  • After three more quiet days, the party finds the lack of encounters and other travelers unnerving. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (travel)
  • Roon notices strange shadows moving on their own around the caravan and warns the party. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (discovery)
  • Gildas investigates the moving shadows and senses faint necrotic divination magic from the Abyssal plane before four lights fly away and the magic disappears. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (discovery)
  • Mikani speaks with plants and senses no buildings or constructed structures nearby, but detects fey and undead presence somewhere in the caravan, Abyssal influence all around, and both celestial and fiendish influences within the party. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (discovery)
  • Corvinas speaks with the caravan rangers and observes that they are an archer-heavy force with beautifully crafted longbows, light armor, and a professional, standoffish leader. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (social)
  • Faban notices Sam, a suspicious man in a rickety carriage at the back of the caravan, and speaks with him about the archaeological dig site. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (social)
  • Sam warns that if the archaeological site's protection is removed, it may bring death, the return of the arcane, the return of the Wells, and many ancient curses. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (discovery)
  • Sam shows Faban a small green glass ring imbued with strange energy and says he intends to give it to the quartermaster. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (discovery)
  • After two more days of travel, the caravan continues along the road toward the archaeological dig site. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (travel)
  • Faban researches Sam's green glass ring through his codex of songs and recalls a long-form poem describing it as a powerful protective ring. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (discovery)
  • Faban attempts to steal the protective green glass ring from Sam at the back of the caravan. [Road to Archaeological Dig Site] (acquisition)
