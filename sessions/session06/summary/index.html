Canon Events

  • The party attacks a large lizard that is attacking the edge of the Fey forest, and Roon is injured in the fight. The large lizard is a dinosaur that has been terrorizing the area, and the party is able to defeat it after a tough battle. Roon takes a significant amount of damage, but is able to recover with the help of the party's healer. [Thataways] (combat)
  • The party returned Urgan's Axe at the tree in Thataways. [Thataways] (acquisition)
  • Claris granted the party access to the Well of Magic beneath the tree in Thataways while Roon went to the tavern; the party descended a spiral staircase into a cave where a mist-formed face spoke from the Well. The Well, named Khorag, said it did not know its siblings well, revealed that one sibling was in the heart of the Gale at a monastery of the Open Hand, said it had five siblings, said all Wells are bound to tell the truth, said it was connected to nature and the divine god of nature, said a Well can be closed with the Wand of Wells, named Jennifer as the last known user of that wand, and warned that one of the party was lying by omission. [Thataways] (discovery)
  • Roon talks to an obvious military guard in the tavern about their strategy for protecting Thisaway, and learns that they are planning to fortify the city [Thataways] (social)
  • Bells ring out in the street outside, indicating an attack on the city, and Roon goes with the guards to defend the city. [Thataways] (combat)
  • The party reunites outside the tree and goes inside, where Claris asks them to defend the tree. [Thataways] (social)
  • Ardema of the seven seals appears, looking somewhat beaten up, with a robe of an archmage and a staff of lightning. [Thataways] (discovery)
