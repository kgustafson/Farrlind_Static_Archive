Session 00: The Party forms

Canon Summary

  • Physical date: 2025-02-02
  • In-game date: 1832 AS Apollal 10
  • Primary location: Bentrios
  • Review status: applied, applied 2026-05-05

Canon Events

  • The campaign opens on Farrlind, a continent bordered by northern mountains, the Gale to the south, desert wastelands east and west, and nearby Kippo Archipelago to the northeast with the war-torn city of Nahundra. [Bentrios] (discovery)
  • Bentrios City is introduced as a thriving technological city where warforged entities work for their masters, and Alexander's Inn in Bentrios is a focal point of activity created by Alexander Venrid's tavern chain. [Bentrios] (discovery)
  • Faban has been in Bentrios for three days, is growing restless, and is busking at Alexander's Inn with The One-Legged Lass when the future party begins to gather. [Alexander's Inn] (social)
  • Bridgit, a halfling with the obvious bearing of a thief, enters Alexander's Inn, takes a seat at the bar, ignores Faban's entertainment, and nurses her ale. [Alexander's Inn] (social)
  • Roon, a half-orc fighter and drinking companion of Faban, enters Alexander's Inn, renews his effort to recruit Faban into the city watch, and buys him an ale while Faban switches to one of Roon's favorite songs, Urgan the Brave. [Alexander's Inn] (social)
  • Corvinas, an obvious paladin of Mihira, enters Alexander's Inn, and Makani, a blue dragonborn, quietly drinks at the bar while Faban performs the ballad of Urgan slaying Ryjax. [Alexander's Inn] (social)
  • Gildas, a frustrated-looking elf wizard, enters Alexander's Inn, orders fish tacos and ale, and is later understood to be searching for an artifact. [Alexander's Inn] (social)
  • A warforged waiter in Alexander's Inn glitches and fails; Gildas and Corvinas investigate and learn similar failures have been happening across the land for months, and Faban's own card sorter promptly fails and burns cards he won from the Duke of Westerbrook. [Alexander's Inn] (discovery)
  • A city watch guard rushes in looking for Roon, reporting issues throughout Bentrios and urgent need at headquarters; Roon quickly enlists Faban, Corvinas, Bridgit, Makani, and Gildas to help. [Alexander's Inn] (social)
  • The newly formed party rushes into the streets of Bentrios with Roon to help, and a large rock-being attacks them, beginning the battle that continues into session 01. [Bentrios] (combat)
