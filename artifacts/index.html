Wells of Magic World Item Session 0 — sentient The Wells of Magic are ancient, sentient or semi-sentient sources of primordial and elemental power. They are not merely reservoirs of magic; they appear tied to the balance of the world, the Cataclysm cycle, and the consequences of mortals or outsiders attempting to control forces that exceed ordinary magic. - There are six Wells of Magic. - Wells are bound to tell the truth. - The Wand of Wells is the only known method to close or control a Well. - The Wand of Wells was last known to be held by Jennifer Wilbreta and is currently missing. - A Cataclysm can be delayed, but may not be fully stoppable. - The Wells are connected to elemental and primordial forces, including Order, Chaos, and Arcana.
