Session 00

The Party forms

x
Real Date
2025-02-02
In World
1832 AS Apollal 10
Primary Location
Bentrios

Major Events

  1. discovery Bentrios Bentrios City is introduced as a thriving technological city where warforged entities work for their masters, and Alexander's Inn in Bentrios is a focal point of activity created by Alexander Venrid's tavern chain.
  2. social Alexander's Inn Bridgit, a halfling with the obvious bearing of a thief, enters Alexander's Inn, takes a seat at the bar, ignores Faban's entertainment, and nurses her ale.
  3. social Alexander's Inn Roon, a half-orc fighter and drinking companion of Faban, enters Alexander's Inn, renews his effort to recruit Faban into the city watch, and buys him an ale while Faban switches to one of Roon's favorite songs, Urgan the Brave.
  4. social Alexander's Inn Corvinas, an obvious paladin of Mihira, enters Alexander's Inn, and Makani, a blue dragonborn, quietly drinks at the bar while Faban performs the ballad of Urgan slaying Ryjax.
  5. social Alexander's Inn Gildas, a frustrated-looking elf wizard, enters Alexander's Inn, orders fish tacos and ale, and is later understood to be searching for an artifact.
  6. discovery Alexander's Inn A warforged waiter in Alexander's Inn glitches and fails; Gildas and Corvinas investigate and learn similar failures have been happening across the land for months, and Faban's own card sorter promptly fails and burns cards he won from the Duke of Westerbrook.
  7. social Alexander's Inn A city watch guard rushes in looking for Roon, reporting issues throughout Bentrios and urgent need at headquarters; Roon quickly enlists Faban, Corvinas, Bridgit, Makani, and Gildas to help.
  8. combat Bentrios The newly formed party rushes into the streets of Bentrios with Roon to help, and a large rock-being attacks them, beginning the battle that continues into session 01.

Session 01

The Battle

x
Real Date
2025-02-16
In World
1832 AS Apollal 10
Primary Location
Bentrios

Major Events

  1. combat Bentrios The party fights a regenerating construct-like creature in Bentrios; Faban is nearly mortally wounded, Corvinas smites it with Mihira's holy energy, and Bridgit lands the final sneak attack that destroys it.
  2. discovery Bentrios Gildas detects magic in the rubble, Faban retrieves a round red orb glowing orange, and Gildas determines that it was planted, uses long-lost magic, and contains evocation magic.
  3. discovery Bentrios Gildas also senses strong protective and concealing magic from Faban's leather satchel, and Faban becomes defensive, insisting that it is his burden to bear.
  4. discovery Bentrios In a nearby building's basement room, Gildas gazes into the orb, sees a fiery realm resembling the Nine Hells and the Demon Lord of Rage, then breaks contact and drops the orb, cracking it and releasing some of its contents before it is mended.
  5. discovery Bentrios The party decides to seek Mayor Baron Wells at Bentrios Tower; as a tempest forms, Makani realizes the storm is unnatural and caused by the Demon Lord of Lightning bringing it down on the city.
  6. discovery Bentrios The party reaches the tilted Bentrios Tower, rushes to the mayor's penthouse, finds the door ajar, and overhears infernal speech: Baron Wells says he has done all he can, while a second voice demands that he has five days to get what is needed.
  7. discovery Bentrios Roon charges into the room, the demon lord calls him a foolish mortal, disappears in a flash, and the ground shifts as Bentrios Tower rights itself.
  8. discovery Bentrios The party interrogates Baron Wells, who says demons are collecting items from around the Gale and seek Urgan the Great's lost axe, also known as the Blood Rage Great Axe of Urgan, which is rumored to be infused with Urgan's rage energy and hidden underground.
  9. discovery Bentrios Faban recalls an ancient ballad saying Urgan's bride, Jennifer Wilbreta, a pale elf of the Fey, traveled south from the northern mountains and buried Urgan's axe somewhere in the central plains.
  10. discovery Bentrios Baron Wells is constrained by vows but confirms that the axe is definitely underground and shows the party a map pointing to an unexplored forest teeming with Fey Wild magic.

Travel

  • Bentrios -> Fey Woods foot , 1 day high Diary dates span Apollal 12 to Apollal 13; inferred as 1 elapsed calendar day.

Major Events

  1. social Bentrios The party visits the Temple of Knowledge in Bentrios, seeks Father Joseph, and asks him about the infernal orb, the animated construct, the storms, Salazar, and the Great Axe of Urgan.
  2. discovery Bentrios Father Joseph says the orb is likely tied to the Demon Lord of Rage, says powerful magic may be controlled by another being like a marionette artist, and explains that self-aware construct magic is rarer and stronger than ordinary somatic construct magic.
  3. discovery Bentrios Father Joseph believes Salazar, the Demon Lord of Lightning, is probably separate from the Demon Lord of Rage seen in the orb; he recommends carrying the orb in a padded case and says the Fey Woods are the best place to look for Urgan's Axe.
  4. travel Bentrios On Apollal 12, the party leaves Bentrios for the Fey Wilds, seeing how the city's machinery has failed and older practices like sundials and oil lamps have returned; along the road they encounter a blacksmith trying to rouse a failed warforged, and he tells them Urgan's Axe was crafted by dwarves a millennium ago.
  5. social Road to Fey Woods On Apollal 13, during the second day of travel, the party meets an old man near the Fey Wilds who warns them not to make deals with the fey, mentions a pack of gnolls about a half-day from the woods, trades riddles with Faban, and gives him a potion of healing.
  6. social Fey Woods Near the outer Fey Woods, Faban tries to light a fire while the party discusses blazing a trail, and a nearby tree strikes him; the tree is Oak, a dryad, who says she is bound by a vow of silence about the axe but warns that fey are hostile to outsiders.
  7. discovery Fey Woods Oak says a hag or gnoll might know more, suggests seeking a satyr or centaur for safe passage, and directs the party toward a lightning-struck tree and then west to a lake where they may find a centaur.
  8. social Fey Woods The next morning, a blue-skinned fey creature tells the party she knows about Urgan's Axe but wants them to lead gnolls to the Satyr camp in exchange; the party refuses, and she brandishes her claws as combat begins.

Major Events

  1. social Thataways The birdfolk wizard escorted the party to a partial sage council where the witch sage and centaur druid heard about the hag's plan to push gnolls into attacking the village, the demonic god Yongu, the infernal orb, and the party's search for Urgan's Axe.

Session 05

The Return to Bentrios

x
Real Date
2025-03-30
In World

Primary Location
Bentrios

Travel

  • Thataways -> Bentrios foot , 1 day high Diary dates span Apollal 14 to Apollal 15; inferred as 1 elapsed calendar day, though the travel covers parts of both dated days.

Major Events

  1. discovery Bentrios The party reached Bentrios, where tower renovations were underway, magic shops were affected by world changes, and technological disruption had caused civilized chaos.
  2. social Bentrios At the temple of Siath, Gildas told Father Joseph the party had recovered Urgan's Axe, discussed whether the axe was connected to Salazar or the Demon Lord of Rage, and the party encouraged Father Joseph to take leadership from the corrupt mayor.
  3. social Bentrios At the mayor's office, the party demanded Baron Wells return Urgan's Axe when he was done with it; he said he would try and admitted his political power, economy, military choices, and foreign dealings were failing.
  4. discovery Bentrios Lightning struck in the room and Salazar appeared, examined Urgan's Axe, recited an incantation, and revealed he was bound by an infernal contract to fix things for Baron Wells in exchange for access to artifacts including the axe.
  5. discovery Bentrios Salazar fulfilled the contract by shunting Bentrios back to its most prosperous Age of Discovery state; Alexander's Inn disappeared, the tower collapsed into the old town square, people were confused, warforged vanished, and Salazar disappeared with a wink.
  6. acquisition Bentrios Faban vowed to capture the fall and contract of Baron Wells in a ballad titled The Contract of Baron Wells.
  7. discovery Bentrios Father Joseph explained that Wells of Magic had opened, that similar events were once called the Cataclysm, that the Wells are potent sources of magic, and that the party must learn how to close the Wells from the Wells themselves.
  8. discovery Bentrios Father Joseph said Hanedal Island has one of the Wells, that there are no known maps to it, and that the Cataclysm forms from natural storms created under unnatural circumstances.
  9. discovery Bentrios Faban connected Father Joseph's description of unnatural storm-cataclysm to the destruction of the outer Celestial Isles where Makani's people originated.
  10. discovery Bentrios Father Joseph said the Alliance of Stars is based in Bentrios and is a small adventuring party; Faban sent a query through his underground network for information on the group.

Travel

  • Archaeological Dig Site -> Mountain Road foot , 0 days medium Session13 begins at the archaeological dig site on Namal 6; the party sets out northward into the mountains and takes shelter in a shallow cave.
  • Mountain Road -> Druid Retreat foot , 4 days high Diary dates span Namal 6 to Namal 10; inferred as 4 elapsed calendar days.

Major Events

  1. acquisition Archaeological Dig Site Gildas shows the obsidian hooked dagger to the archeologists; the blade is made for tearing flesh and has dried black blood on it, and Gildas stores it with the orb fragments and the Bentrios orb.
  2. social Archaeological Dig Site The party estimates it will take two to four days to reach the druid retreat, camps near the dig site, and Corvinas tells the party about being the last survivor of Mistrona after a demon attack.
  3. discovery Archaeological Dig Site Corvinas describes the demon claw mark on his forehead; Roon recognizes the crossed-axes symbol as belonging to Polachnicindoya, lord of the seventh level of hell and demon of rage.
  4. discovery Mountain Road Cole appears at the cave with a magical deck of cards; Faban draws The Rogue, which Cole says harbingers betrayal, and Roon draws The Star, which signifies growth and evolution and makes him stronger.
  5. discovery Mountain Road Mikani communes with nature in the mountains and senses the conspicuous absence of planar influence, no powerful beings influencing the area, traces of amethyst, iron, and obsidian in the earth, and residual evocation magic from spells cast long ago.
  6. discovery Druid Retreat As the party nears an encampment, vines trap Corvinas and an unseen archer fires a warning arrow with a parchment reading, "Those who seek, harm, or burn, shall face the wrath of many here."
  7. social Druid Retreat Faban calls out in greeting, and an ancient elvish archer named Jennifer appears, revealing herself as the object of the party's quest.
  8. discovery Druid Retreat Jennifer says she no longer has the Wand of Wells because it was stolen recently amid broader magical failures and unreliable protection spells.
  9. discovery Druid Retreat Jennifer identifies the known Wells: the Monastery of the Open Hand, Thataway, Henedal, the beaches to the east at Paramon, the sunken city of Catur, and the dwarven city of Balrog.
  10. discovery Druid Retreat Jennifer explains that the Wells are kind, do not seek retribution, and are not inherently evil, and that stopping the Cataclysm requires seeking all of the Wells.
  11. social Druid Retreat Jennifer leads the party into the Druid Retreat, also described as her coven, welcomes them to her patch of earth, says the situation is complicated, and notes that although she is over a thousand years old, many in the retreat can help the party.
  12. discovery Druid Retreat Jennifer warns the party not to put full trust in the gods because the gods are flawed much like mortals.
