Ranger Rick and His Mighty Stick
(A rowdy tavern boast song)
[Intro – Leader]
Now gather ‘round and fill your cup—
[Crowd]
HEY!
[Leader]
And listen while we sing this up!
[Crowd]
HEY!
[Verse 1]
There once was a ranger, lean and quick,
Name of Rick and a mighty stick.
Through woods so dark and roads so wild,
He’d face the beasts and grin and smile.
[Pre-Chorus – Call & Response]
Who keeps the night from getting thick?
[Crowd]
RANGER RICK!
[Leader]
What does he swing that does the trick?
[Crowd]
HIS MIGHTY STICK!
[Chorus]
OH RICK! RICK!
THE MIGHTY STICK!
Swings so fast it lands so quick!
OH RICK! RICK!
DON’T YOU MISS!
Every blow lands just like this!
(HEY!)
________________


[Verse 2]
A troll once rose by the river bend,
Thought that Rick had met his end.
But Rick just laughed and took his stance—
Said, “Let’s you and me have a little dance.”
[Pre-Chorus – Call & Response]
What knocked that troll into the creek?
[Crowd]
RANGER RICK!
[Leader]
What made the whole damn mountain shake?
[Crowd]
HIS MIGHTY STICK!
[Chorus]
OH RICK! RICK!
THE MIGHTY STICK!
Swings so fast it lands so quick!
OH RICK! RICK!
DON’T YOU MISS!
Every blow lands just like this!
(HEY!)
[Verse 3]
Some say it’s oak, some say it’s steel,
Some say it’s how you use the deal…
But all agree when Rick lets fly—
You’ll hear the crack before you cry.
[Optional Bawdy Tag]
And if you doubt what we all say—
[Crowd]
TRY IT YOURSELF—BUT NOT TODAY!
________________


[Final Chorus]
OH RICK! RICK!
THE MIGHTY STICK!
Swings so hard it does the trick!
OH RICK! RICK!
RAISE YOUR DRINK!
You’ll be down before you think!
(HEY!)
