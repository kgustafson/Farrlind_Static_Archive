# **Mihira's Rise** **(The Ballad of Justice Untamed)**

**\[Verse 1\]**

Beneath the skies of burning gold  
Where whispered legends still are told  
A goddess stood with veil and blade  
Mihira, judge whom none betrayed

Her scales once weighed the truth alone  
Blind to crown and blind to throne  
Yet fate would weave a tale so grand  
Of gods who sought her timeless hand

**\[Pre-Chorus\]**

Wurrushka came with war in stride  
Run the Sun burned fierce with pride  
Two divine hearts fierce and bright  
Would tear the heavens in their fight

**\[Chorus\]**

Oh Mihira, goddess of night and day  
Blind no longer, so the mortals say  
Did you see the fire, the war, the light  
And trade your justice for mortal sight?

Oh Mihira, your balance may sway  
But truth still cuts in a ruthless way

**\[Verse 2\]**

Wurrushka thundered from the field  
His scars the trophies battles yield  
“Take my hand and rule with might  
Together we will guard the right.”

But Run approached with rising dawn  
His voice as warm as morning sun  
“With me the world will stand in grace  
For justice shines within your face.”

**\[Pre-Chorus\]**

Steel met flame on moonlit tide  
Where love and fury both collide  
The heavens wept with falling flame  
And when she saw, she was not the same

**\[Chorus\]**

Oh Mihira, goddess of truth and might  
When you saw the war did it steal your sight?  
The blindfold fell and the stars did gleam  
Was it a curse or a shattered dream?

Oh Mihira, your power untold  
But is justice still fair with eyes of gold?

**\[Bridge\]**

Now whispers echo through mortal halls  
Of judgment flawed and justice that falls  
For sight brings doubt where none once stood  
And mercy walks where steel once would

Yet Mihira stands with sword in hand  
Her will as firm as ancient land  
Still walking paths where truths must meet  
Between the sun and war's heartbeat

**\[Final Chorus\]**

Oh Mihira, goddess of night and day  
Blind no longer, so the mortals say  
Yet even flawed your justice stands  
The sharpest blade in mortal hands

Oh Mihira, through time you rise  
Justice seeing through mortal eyes

**\[Outro\]**

Blind or seeing, her legend burns  
As star and age forever turn  
For justice walks where mortals climb  
And Mihira’s name outlives all time
